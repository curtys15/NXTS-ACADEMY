All about Us

Next Step Academy was created in 1990 by the 3x NBA Champion,2x MVP of the year,2000-2001 Rookie of the year, Nge Okwe Curtys who created this academy in order to introduce to young talents the basics and significant pillars for a succesfull and worthy sport career. Inspired by the Mamba Mentality which was created and imagined by the basketball legend and franchise legend of the Los Angeles Lakers Kobe Bryant,Nge wanted to provide a healthy and well-balanced environment which can provide professional trainings in Basketball,Volley ball,Netball,Pingpong,Hockey,Soccer,swimming and any other international sport.

Our Promises

NXTS Academy is a place where ingenious teachers, intrepid students, and forward-thinking leaders work together to shape an education on the adventurous edge. Teachers create meaningful, hands-on learning experiences in the classroom, on the stage, on the playing field and court, and on life-changing journeys. Graduates are primed to excel at the finest colleges and universities, forge purposeful careers, and employ their unshakable optimism to improve the wider world.
